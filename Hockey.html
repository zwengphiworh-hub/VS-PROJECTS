Hockey Rules

  • Hockey is played with two teams of 6 players each.
  • The objective is to score points by shooting the puck into the opponent's goal.
  • Players can only move the puck using their sticks, not their hands.
  • Physical contact is allowed, but players must follow specific rules to avoid penalties.
  • The game is divided into two halves, each lasting 40 minutes.

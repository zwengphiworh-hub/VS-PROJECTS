Cricket Rules

  • Cricket is played with two teams of 11 players each.
  • The objective is to score runs by hitting the ball and running between the wickets.
  • Players can hit the ball with their bat, but cannot use their hands (except for the bowler).
  • The game is divided into two innings, each lasting a specified number of overs.
